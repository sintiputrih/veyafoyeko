Holly Conrad’s Early Life and Career:

  • Birth and Education: Holly Conrad was born on May 22, 1986, in Los Angeles, California, making her 33 years old. She attended the University of Santa Barbara and later studied at the University of Edinburgh, specializing in folk magic and Medieval literature.
  • Cosplay and YouTube: Holly became known for her cosplay creations, particularly her portrayal of “Commander Shepard” from the Mass Effect series. She worked with Bioware as the official Commander Shepard in 2011 and 2012, designing various outfits based on the series. She also gained recognition for her special FX work, including creating a Kaiju for the promotional video “Pacific Rim – Training Day” and crafting creatures for “The Four Players” video series.
  • DnD and Twitch: Holly is also a member of the DnD group called Dice, Camera, Action, which streams on Twitch every Tuesday. The group includes other notable personalities like Nathan Sharp and Jared Knabenbauer.

Holly Conrad’s Personal Life:

  • Marriage and Divorce: Holly was previously married to Ross O’Donovan, an Australian animator and Internet personality. They tied the knot on September 29, 2012, after dating for some time. However, they announced their divorce on September 19, 2018, with the main reason being Conrad’s relocation to Seattle, Washington.
  • Relationship with Jared Knabenbauer: Following her divorce, Holly began a relationship with Jared “ProJared” Knabenbauer, a well-known YouTube gamer. However, their relationship became controversial when Jared’s ex-wife, Heidi O’Ferrall, accused him of infidelity with Holly. The revelation led to a significant loss of subscribers for Jared’s YouTube channel, and Holly also faced criticism from her own followers.
