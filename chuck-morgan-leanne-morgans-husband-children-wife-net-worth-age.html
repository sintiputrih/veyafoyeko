Leanne Morgan is an American comedian who was born on 3rd October 1964. She is 59 years old. While Leanne Morgan had a Hollywood dream at a young age, she chose a conventional lifestyle because it was so daunting. So, she began selling jewelry at family parties. Instead of discussing the jewelry, she chose to joke about hemorrhoids, breastfeeding, and the struggles of being a mother. People loved her talk, so she started getting invitations to speak at home parties, eventually becoming a full-time comedian. Her success led to her getting booked at comedy clubs and on television. She quickly rose to fame becoming one of the most popular comedians in the country. She graduated from University of Tennessee.

In Aug 2024, Leanne Morgan will also be making her debut as a writer, What in the World?, which is set to be published by Random House/Convergent.
