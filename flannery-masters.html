Flannery (Japanese: アスナ Asuna) is a Pokémon Trainer in Pokémon Masters EX, based on the character of the same name in the core series games. She forms a sync pair with Torkoal. This sync pair was first made available since the game release.

Trivia

Origin

Flannery's design is based on her appearance in Pokémon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire. Torkoal is Flannery's signature Pokémon in the core series games. Torkoal's Dex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Sun.
