Biography

Olga Lydia was born in Jakarta, Indonesia on December 4, 1976. Actress who is known for appearing in television, film, and as a host on a variety of different programs. She is best known for being the host on the Good Morning series on Trans 7 as well as for playing the character Mrs. Ling-Ling in the 2012 film Soegija. She starred alongside Lala Karmela and Amel Carla in the 2015 film Ngenest. She graduated from the Parahyangan University Bandung with a degree in Civil Engineering. Upon graduation, she began working as a model for commercials and runways. She made her first television appearance in the series Lo Fen Koei and went on to make her film debut in 2005 in the film 12:00am as the character Inez.
