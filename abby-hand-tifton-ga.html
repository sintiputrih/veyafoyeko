Abby Hand Tifton GA: Abby Rivers Hand Car Accident

Tifton, GA – In a heartbreaking incident that has left the community in mourning, Abby Rivers Hand, a 20-year-old student at Abraham Baldwin Agricultural College (ABAC), lost her life in a car accident on November 25, 2023. This devastating news has shaken not only her family and friends but also the ABAC community where she was pursuing her education.
