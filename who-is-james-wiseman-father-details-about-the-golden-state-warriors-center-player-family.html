Who Is James Wiseman Father And Mother Donzaleigh Artis? More On His Parents

James Wiseman was born to his estranged parents, father Stefan Wiseman and mother Donzaleigh Artis.

As his mother raised him and his siblings single-handedly, the NBA star has almost no connection with his father.

Donzaleigh is a single mother who went to lengths to support young James’ basketball career.

NBC Sports writes that Ms. Artis worked two jobs to pay the bills and put food on the family’s table. She did whatever she could to provide for the athlete and keep him on the right path.

When the Warriors selected James in the 2020 NBA draft, he first told his mother that she would come to San Francisco with him and never work for a day from that day onward.

James finally pulled off his mother from her hardships and is giving her the restful life she deserves.

NBA Player James Wiseman Sibling Jaquarius Greer

James Wiseman is the only surviving son of his mother, along with his sibling, an elder sister named Jaquarius Greer. 

San Franciso Chronicle reports that Donzaleigh had three children, including James and his elder sister, Jaquarius.

The NBA mother lost her other son, Bobby Ray, when he was five years old in 1995. The late Bobby had an accident at an apartment complex swimming pool despite being under family care.

Ray passed away two days later at the hospital. Since then, Donzaleigh has taken care of her other two children more closely than ever not to go through the same situation.

Hence, growing under the love and care of their responsible mother, James and Jaquarius have grown up to be responsible adults.

Jaquarius is a graduate of the University of Memphis. She is available on Instagram under the username @_jaquarius_g.
