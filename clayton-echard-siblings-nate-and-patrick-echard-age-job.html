Meet Patrick Echard, Clayton Echard Brother

Patrick Echard is the youngest brother of Clayton and Nate Echard.

  • Patrick Echard Age

Born in 1997, Patrick Echard is 25 years old (as of 2022).

  • Patrick Echard Job

In 2014, Patrick Echard started as a Volunteer. He worked for Eureka Park & Recreation, Ramay Junior High, and the City of Eureka, Missouri from 2014 to 2017. From 2016 to 2017, he worked for Under Armour as a sales/stock teammate. For three months in 2018, he also worked for Hansel Paiting LLC as a painter.

Furthermore, in 2019, Patrick worked for University Hospital as Research Assistant followed by Infection Control Intern.

He had also worked for Harpo’s Bar & Grill as Security Personnel, Bar Specialist, and then Bartender (August 2016 – May 2020). He worked for Aerotek as a medical screener on a contract basis for four months in 2020.

Patrick joined Loco Patron as Busser/ Food Runner/ Barback. From September 2020 to November 2020, he worked for Wright Medical as Hub Material Handler.

As of March 2022, Patrick has been working for Stryker as an Upper Extremities Sales Associate starting from December 2021. He had initially joined the company as Hub Material Handler.

Coming to his academic qualification, Patrick earned his Bachelor’s degree in Health Science from the University of Arkansas with a 4.0 GPA and a Bachelor’s in Health Science, Field Of StudyRehabilitation Sciences; a Minor in Psychology with a 3.6 GPA.
