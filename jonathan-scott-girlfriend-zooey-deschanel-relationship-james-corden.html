How Did James Corden Bring Zooey Deschanel And Jonathan Scott Together?

After serving as arguably one of the best talk show hosts of all time, Craig Ferguson disappointed his fans when he announced that he was stepping away from his late-night show. After Ferguson's final show, James Corden was tapped to replace him.

In the years since The Late Late Show with James Corden premiered, the show's host has unfortunately garnered a lot of hate. Despite that, there is no denying that Corden's late-night talk show has enjoyed a lot of success.

At the height of The Late Late Show with James Corden's popularity, the Carpool Karaoke segment became a sensation. In fact, the segment was so beloved that it got its own spin-off show, Carpool Karaoke: The Series.

Back when Carpool Karaoke was just a recurring segment on The Late Late Show with James Corden, major musicians were a staple. Once Carpool Karaoke: The Series premiered, however, the show's producers needed to widen the net.

As a result of the musical parameters being loosened, the Property Brothers Drew and Jonathan Scott starred in an episode of Carpool Karaoke: The Series. During the same episode, Emily and Zooey Deschanel appeared.

As it turns out, Zooey Deschanel and Jonathan Scott met during the filming of that Carpool Karaoke: The Series episode. After hitting it off, the pair began dating and the rest is history.

Even though James Corden doesn't appear onscreen during most Carpool Karaoke: The Series episodes, he plays a huge role in the show. After all, Corden is credited as a Carpool Karaoke: The Series' co-creator and executive producer.

At this point, there is no way to know if James Corden has any ongoing role in which celebrities appear on Carpool Karaoke: The Series. However, Carpool Karaoke: The Series never would have existed in the first place if it wasn't for Corden.

Whether or not James Corden approved Zooey Deschanel and Jonathan Scott appearing in the same episode, the couple met due to Carpool Karaoke: The Series. Since that show never would have existed if it wasn't for Corden, he played a major role in them becoming a couple.

The fact that Jonathan Scott and Zooey Deschanel's relationship survived that kind of pressure cooker says a lot about their connection. As it turns out, that was far from the only indication that Scott and Deschanel were very serious about each other.
