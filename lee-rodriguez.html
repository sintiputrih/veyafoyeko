Acting is something Lee Rodriguez took up later on in her life, her first love and something she is still actively pursuing is her desire to be a renowned singer. She was only a kid when Lee realized she could sing, and her parents were also supportive of her talents. The actress cannot remember a specific moment where she looked at herself, and though she wanted to be a singer, Lee said it just came to her, singing along with the TV and music records.

Lee Rodriguez was born on 28 November 1999 to her parents in California. She recently celebrated her 20th birthday and gave a glimpse of her celebration on her Instagram page. A graduated of the Encore High School, located in California after she was done with her training and education, the actress signed with her manager and agent and started on her pursuit to be a successful singer and also star in movies and TV shows.

The resident of California is a newbie to the world of acting, we could only find two credits to her name on IMDb, and both were in 2018. Playing a character in the upcoming highly-anticipated series on Netflix is definitely going to bring some eyeballs to her and her still budding career.

Lee Rodriguez Plays Fabiola in the Netflix Series Never Have I Ever

Never Have I Ever is the newest addition to the immense original library of digital content of the streaming giant Netflix. A semi-autobiographical work by talented actress/writer Mindy Kaling deals with the struggle of a TikTok generation South-Asian teen who finds it hard to adjust with her western friends and the cultural side of her family and relatives.

Lee Rodriguez plays Fabiola in the new series; she is one of the two friends of lead character Devi, played by Maitreyi Ramakrishnan. Fabiola and Devi and Eleanor, played by Ramona Young, hang together and are destined to not be the nerds of the school when it is all said and done. They are also Devi’s confidant when it comes to her sexual desires and everything in between.

Lee Rodriguez plays Fabiola, one of Devi's friends, in the Netflix series Never Have I Ever.
Source: Lee Rodriguez Instagram

Never Have I Ever is one of the highly anticipated series of this year, coming from an acclaimed writer who is known for pushing Indian-subcontinent characters on screen and providing a platform of representation for characters which are extremely stereotyped in Hollywood. The casting process for the show was also extensive; Devi’s role was cast from a pool of 15,000 aspirant South-Asian actresses.

The new Netflix series, Never Have I Ever, will be released on 27 April 2020, starring Maitreyi Ramakrishnan as Devi, Poorna Jagannathan as Nalini, Sendhil Ramamurthy as Mohan, Richa Moorjani as Kamala, Ramona Young as Eleanor, Lee Rodriguez as Fabiola, Darren Barnet as Paxton Hall-Yoshida, and Jaren Lewison as Ben Gross.

In 10 Years Lee Rodriguez wants to Release an Album and Go on Tour

https://www.instagram.com/p/Bj1DZpwD4nv/

Before her acting career took off from the ground, Lee Rodriguez saw herself as a successful touring artist. In a 2018 interview, the actress said she wants to go on tour in and also drop an album in the next ten years. She definitely has some singing prowess, the actress sang during her graduation ceremony, and she frequently posts singing videos on her Instagram page.

She also started learning to play guitar in 2018 after her manager and agent got her the instrument for her birthday. Lee is pretty good on the guitar now, but when it comes to her voice, it is her strong suit. When asked about nerves while performing, she said there are little nerves onstage, she gets afraid before going on, but performing takes all the fear away.

The Fabiola actress recounted a time when she was around six or seven years old when she performed for a small crowd, standing at the top of a staircase. She wanted to perform inside a bar, but to get inside, she needed to be at least 21; instead, the manager allowed her to sing near the entrance of the hotel.

Lee Rodriguez joined Instagram in 2015, but looking at her post count does not give that away. When it comes to posting about her relationship on or any personal detail, the actress is even more tightlipped. A beautiful lady by the name of Tira frequented her Instagram page in the 2018s, but what to what extent their relationship went is a mystery. It seems the actress is currently single and ready to further both her singing and acting career simultaneously.
