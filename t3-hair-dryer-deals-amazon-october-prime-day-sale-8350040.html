There are so many reasons to love Amal Clooney. She’s a phenomenal lawyer that's married to everyone’s favorite actor, and she doesn’t miss a beat when it comes to her glam — the epitome of beauty and brains. But the secret to Clooney’s classic Hollywood tresses, according to her stylist Rod Ortega, is the brand T3 Micro’s iconic hair tools. And it’s just our luck that its T3 Airebrush — aka its interchangeable hot air blow dry brush  — is currently on rare sale for Amazon’s October Prime Day, which means you, too, can get never-out-of-place strands like Clooney at home.

The blow dryer brush boasts three heat settings, two-speed options, and a lock-in cool shot comprising 15 heat and speed combinations. The built-in ion generator allows for precise and digitally controlled heat for a frizz-free and shiny finish. It comes with two interchangeable attachments, including a round brush for voluminous styles or a paddle brush attachment that works on curly hair textures and for those looking to achieve a sleek, straight finish. The hair dryer's design is smaller and lighter than standard dryers, making it ideal for complex styling and travel.

I don’t think much convincing is needed after seeing how Clooney’s strands never seem to be out of place on both the red carpet and in the courtroom, but if you need a little extra push, these raving Amazon reviews should do it. One shopper with fine hair said after using the paddle brush attachment, their hair had “volume” and was “silky smooth” with “less damage” than their flat iron. 

Another reviewer said the hair dryer brush “outperformed” against other pricier brands and is “great for thick hair.” They also mentioned that the hair dryer was still “effective,” even with larger chunks of hair. They said the trick is to go in with the “correct heat setting for your hair type, and create adequate tension on the hair strands as you brush them out” to get a “salon quality blowout.”
