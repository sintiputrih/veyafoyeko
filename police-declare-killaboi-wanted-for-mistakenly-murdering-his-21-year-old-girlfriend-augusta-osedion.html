Wanted: Killaboi, Nigerian Man Accused of Fatally Stabbing 21-Year-Old Girlfriend

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  198 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The Nigerian Police Force is actively seeking Killaboi in relation to the tragic death of 21-year-old Augusta Osedion, his girlfriend. The Lagos State Police Command released an official statement on October 10, announcing the search for the 26-year-old suspect.

In a surprising turn of events, Killaboi admitted to stabbing his fiancée, Augusta Osedion, under what he described as a “mistaken” action during a disagreement in July. He revealed his state of distress and suicidal thoughts following the incident.

According to the police statement, “The Lagos State Police Command hereby declares Benjamin Best Nnanyereugo wanted. Nnanyereugo is a suspect in the murder of his girlfriend, 21-year-old Augusta Onuwabhagbe Osedion, which occurred on July 13, 2023, in his Oral Estate, Ajah residence.”

The announcement continued, “After exhausting all available avenues to bring him in, he is now declared a wanted man. The suspect, aged 26, stands at approximately 5’9 ft tall, hails from Abia State, has a dark complexion, and is fluent in English, Igbo, and Pidgin English.”

Despite the efforts of Benjamin Hundeyin, the Lagos State Police Command spokesperson, Killaboi, who identifies as a blockchain developer and crypto enthusiast, has evaded authorities since the tragic incident.
