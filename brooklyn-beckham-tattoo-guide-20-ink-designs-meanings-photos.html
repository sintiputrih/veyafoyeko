Brooklyn Beckham Tattoo Guide: Ink Designs, Meanings, Photos

 Posted on January 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  416 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

A tattooed king! Brooklyn Beckham has followed in his father’s footsteps when it comes to tattoos. Throughout his time in the spotlight, the budding photographer — whose parents are soccer star David Beckham and former Spice Girl Victoria Beckham — has racked up over 20 ink designs all over his body. Since his dad has over 60, it’s no surprise that Brooklyn would want to follow suit!

Following his engagement to longtime love Nicola Peltz, Brooklyn got multiple tattoos dedicated to his fiancée. Celebrity tattoo artist Doctor Woo shared his latest ink, eyes on the back of his neck above some coordinates, via Instagram in October 2020. “Eyes see love,” he captioned the post, tagging both Brooklyn and Nicola. In January 2021, Brooklyn added another ink design dedicated to his lovely lady to his growing collection. This time he got a love letter she wrote him tattooed on his back.

“My forever boy. Read this anytime you feel anxious. I want you to know how deeply loved you are. You have the kindest heart I’ve ever met and I hope I never go a day without your love,” it reads. “I think you are so incredible. Just know we can get through it all together if you breathe slow and trust. I love you beyond.”

The couple made their relationship Instagram official in January 2020 after they were first spotted together in October 2019. Since then, they’ve shared their love all over the internet by posting tons of PDA-filled pics. In fact, Nicola even called Brooklyn the “love of her life” when celebrating his birthday in March 2020. Months later, in July 2020, the couple announced their via Instagram.

“She always cries when I get her another tattoo,” Brooklyn told USA Today in August 2022, revealing that he has 70 dedicated to his now-wife. “I always love to surprise her with new ink.”

Aside from the tattoos dedicated to his other half, Brooklyn has also racked up a few that act as heartfelt tributes to his three siblings and parents. It’s safe to say he has some pretty meaningful messages permanently inked on him, including a few matching ones with his father. But what do all his tattoo designs mean? Well, J-14 did a major investigation and broke down all of Brooklyn’s ink designs.

From what he’s said about them to what fans think they mean, scroll through our gallery for a complete breakdown of all Brooklyn’s tattoos.
