REAL Housewives of Beverly Hills star Erika Jayne has fans divided over how they feel about her new topless photos.

The Bravo star bared all in a photoshoot with Interview Magazine, who shared the sultry pics on Instagram.

One photo showed Erika, 52, sans top, using her arm and giant bracelets to cover her breasts.

She completed her look with large hoop earrings, heavy eye makeup, and her platinum locks flowing straight down.

Another snap showed the Real Housewives star donning a skintight, plunging, black Bluemarine dress with a thick leather belt to highlight her tiny waist amid her drastic weight loss.

She again let her hair fall past her shoulders and flashed her bright red manicured tips at the camera.

And one more captured Erika rocking a black body-hugging leather Balmain suit, with gloves and a black headband.

The ensemble exposed her shoulder and neck as her hair was styled in a low bun.

Interview Magazine revealed in their caption that the photos and a feature story on the RHOBH star would be in their September 2023 issue and included the tagline: "F**k everybody."

FAN BACKLASH

Fans and critics flooded the comments to give their mixed opinions about Erika - who made headlines over her estranged husband Tom Girardi's embezzlement scheme - appearing in the issue.

"Love that her career is built on the $ of orphans and widows SO COOL GUYS," one person wrote.

"Proudly sponsored by the stolen money of burn victims," another harshly stated.

To counter, a supporter commented, "Incredible," and another gushed: "She manifested that."

"Ew, but why?" another chimed in, while a final called Erika a "horrible choice" for the publication.

LEGAL ISSUES

The controversial TV personality has been wrapped up in her husband's legal issues, as many have suspected she was involved.

In February, Tom was charged with eight counts of wire fraud and four counts of criminal contempt of court.

He was accused of allegedly pocketing more than $3million in legal settlement money from family members of Lion Air Flight 610 crash victims.

Erika was not charged with any crimes but received backlash from fans over her lavish lifestyle and was accused of being a "golddigger" in her relationship with her 83-year-old husband.
