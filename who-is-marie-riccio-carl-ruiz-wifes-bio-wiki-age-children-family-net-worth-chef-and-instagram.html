Marie Riccio Bio – Marie Riccio Wiki

Marie Antonia Riccio or Marie Riccio otherwise known  as Marie Kirby from New Jersey is the ex-wife of celebrity Chef Carl Ruiz who was a Cuban American Chef. She is the sole owner and operator of Marie’s Italian Specialties in Jatham New Jersey.

The two founded the Italian Specialities on October 10, 2011 according to the Times, where they shared duties equally although when they divorced Marie Riccio kept the restaurant.

She graduated from Rutgers University – Newark Campus University in Newark New Jersey information found on her LinkedIn profile.
