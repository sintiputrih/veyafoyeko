Emily Ratajkowski Wears a Black Dress Over Her Pantaleggings to Balenciaga's Paris Show

 Posted on June 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  301 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Emily Ratajkowski got the dress code memo and perfected it.

The model arrived at Balenciaga’s Paris Fashion Week show in a slinky all-black outfit, courtesy of the house, that perfectly melded her personal style with Demna’s now-iconic sleek black designs.

The look consisted of a skintight long-sleeved little black dress worn over Balenciaga’s shiny spandex Knife pantaleggings. Continuing the color scheme, EmRata accessorized with a black leather shoulder bag. She kept her makeup minimal and wore her hair straight down to sit front-row at the runway show.

Julien Lienard//Getty Images

Ratajkowski wore another black midi dress in Paris last week. Then, she opted for a romantic design by Tory Burch, which included a corsetlike bustier and a flowy, stretchy skirt.

Today, the model shared an Instagram photo dump featuring candid shots of her latest Paris Fashion Week looks. On one occasion, she wore a beige corset top with brown leather pants. Another day, she stepped out in a little black dress with a daring back cutout.

And on a different day, the My Body author opted for a chic netted brown slipdress. The Dolce & Gabbana midi dress was comprised of a gauzy beige base layer and a brown netted overlay. The piece was semi-sheer and featured thin spaghetti straps, a plunging scoop neckline, and brown floral lace along the hem. Her black underwear was visible through the piece, and she completed the look with coordinating chocolate-brown strappy sandals.
