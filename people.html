Who is Bryce Gheisar? Bio & Age

The child actor was born in Winnipeg Canada on 22. December 2004 under the zodiac sign Capricorn. He is of white ethnicity and Canadian nationality. He was born to Nicole and Todd Gheisar and has a sibling named Blake Gheisar. From what is apparent, Bryce is still in his formative years and is still in the process of formal education. According to our information, he spent his elementary school years at Lovejoy Elementary in Allen. He also attends gymnastics and acting classes.
