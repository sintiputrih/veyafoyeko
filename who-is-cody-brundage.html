Who is Cody Brundage? | Fan Arch

 Posted on June 7, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  715 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Cody Brundage, an American mixed martial artist, was born on May 16, 1994, in Chapin, South Carolina.

His athletic prowess was evident from a young age, marked by his accomplishments as a high school wrestler, football player, and swim team member.

Brundage's exceptional performance in high school wrestling, with a remarkable 56–3 record as a senior and a career record of 128–22, set the stage for his future in combat sports.

His passion for Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) was ignited during his wrestling days, where he organized fight clubs in wrestling rooms, reflecting his early inclination towards competitive combat sports.

Wrestling Career and Transition to MMA

Brundage continued to pursue wrestling at Newberry College, amassing 96 wins and earning the status of a two-time NCAA qualifier as well as a team captain.

His wrestling background laid a solid foundation for his transition into professional MMA.

After competing in college and wrestling for the Newberry College team, Brundage ventured into fighting and training to fulfill his competitive drive, marking the initiation of his journey into the world of MMA.

Professional MMA Career

Brundage made his amateur debut in 2017, eventually turning professional in 2019. His early professional career saw him achieve a 5–0 record on the local Midwest scene, capturing the Lights Out Championship Middleweight and Light Heavyweight Championships. Despite a setback in his bid for a UFC contract at Dana White's Contender Series 31, Brundage continued to showcase his talent and resilience, ultimately leading to his UFC debut.

UFC Debut and Notable Fights

Brundage's UFC journey began with his debut against Nick Maximov at UFC 266, where he faced a tough challenge and lost via unanimous decision.

He quickly rebounded with a notable victory against Dalcha Lungiambula at UFC Fight Night, earning the Performance of the Night award with a win via guillotine choke in the first round.

This victory marked a significant milestone in his UFC career, demonstrating his ability to seize opportunities and deliver compelling performances inside the octagon.

Personal Life and Family Influence

Beyond his professional endeavors, Brundage's family background has played an influential role in shaping his athletic pursuits.

Hailing from an athletic family, his mother's involvement in the U.S. Olympic team for biathlon and his father's service in the Special Forces underscore the strong foundation of determination and athleticism in his upbringing.

His siblings' involvement in sports, with his sister playing soccer at the D-1 level and his youngest brother excelling in lacrosse, reflects the pervasive spirit of athleticism within the Brundage family.

Accomplishments and Recognition

Brundage's professional MMA record stands at 10 wins and 5 losses, showcasing his resilience and determination in a highly competitive arena.

His victories, including impressive finishes through knockout and submission, underscore his multifaceted skill set and adaptability as a fighter.

His participation in UFC events and notable performances have earned him recognition within the MMA community, positioning him as a promising talent with the potential for further growth and success in the future.

Beyond the Octagon: Personal Endeavors

Beyond his athletic pursuits, Brundage's personal life reflects his commitment to family and his role as a husband to former UFC fighter Amanda Brundage.

His wife's decision to retire from professional fighting to pursue a nursing license reflects the values of dedication and support that define their relationship.

This aspect of his life showcases a well-rounded persona, balancing the demands of professional athleticism with personal commitments and aspirations.

A Glimpse into the Future

As Cody Brundage continues to navigate his path in the UFC, his journey serves as a testament to perseverance, athleticism, and personal growth.

With a background rooted in wrestling and a transition to MMA, Brundage has emerged as a compelling figure in the realm of combat sports, driven by a passion for competition and the pursuit of excellence.
