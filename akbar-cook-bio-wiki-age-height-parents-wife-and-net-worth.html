Akbar Cook Career

Cook and Sheridan “Sheri” Cook is a team of Married Educators on The Amazing Race 33. The two often clashed, with Sheri’s slowness in tasks due to her age frustrating Akbar, and Akbar’s frustrations angering Sheri. They consistently found themselves in the back half of the pack, never finishing higher than 5th on any leg.

They met their demise In Leg 6 when Akbar struggled to complete a physical Roadblock, which caused them to slip from fourth to last place. While Akbar and Sheri had some remaining hope to stay in the race after the Roadblock due to Lulu and Lala struggling to put their kayak together, they could not make up the time that they had lost, leading to their elimination at 6th place.
