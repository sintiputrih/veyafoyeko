Personal Life of Shanaya Kapoor

Shanaya Kapoor was born on 3rd November 1999 in Mumbai. Her father Sanjay Kapoor is a Bollywood actor and her mother Maheep Kapoor is a Jewellery designer. She did her schooling at Ecole Mondiale World School, Mumbai. She was born in the most popular Kapoor family of Hindi cinema, her uncle Anil Kapoor is an actor & Boney Kapoor is a film producer.

Bollywood actress Late Shridevi is her aunt, and she was having proper Bollywood cousins where Sonam Kapoor and Jhanvi Kapoor is her cousin sister, and Arjun Kapoor, Harsvardhan Kapoor is her cousin.

She made news headlines with her charismatic debut at Le Bal Des Debutantes in Paris, she wore an alluring red gown by the French-Indian designer label Lecoanet Hemant

Shanaya Kapoor began her Hindi movie carrier in 2020 by working as assistant director in the Netflix movie Gunjan Saxena: The Kargil Girl, in this movie her cousin sister Jhanvi Kapoor, is featured in the lead role. As per the news, she is making her Bollywood debut as an actress in her upcoming movie Bedhadak, directed by popular film director Karan Johar.
