Sam Skarsgård is popularly known as a Swedish physician, actor and production manager and also known as the son of actor Stellan Skarsgard and his first wife, physician My Skarsgard. Most of Sam’s family members are mostly in the film industry. In this post, we will look at what he does and more facts about his personal life.

Sam Skarsgård Biography

Skarsgård was born on 5th June 1982 in Stockholm, Sweden. His parents are actor Stellan Skarsgard and his first wife, physician My Skarsgard. He also has siblings namely Alexander Skarsgard, Bill Skarsgard, Eija Skarsgard, Gustaf Skarsgard, and Valter Skarsgard.

Sam works in the Intensive Care Unit of a Stockholm hospital as a physician. Just like his siblings and father, Sam is also an actor and has been featured in some movies such as “Jim & Piraterna Blom”, “‘The Invisible” and as a production manager on “Att döda ett barn” (2003) and “Skuggvärld” (2005).

Sam Skarsgård Wife

The physician is married to Sanna Skarsgård. His wife has appeared alongside her husband and the rest of the Skarsgård family at various events.

Sam Skarsgård’s Father

Sam’s father, Stellan Skarsgard has appeared in several movies. Some of them are listed below.

  • Breaking the Waves
  • Good Will Hunting
  • Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man’s Chest
  • Pirates of the Caribbean: At World’s End
  • Mamma Mia!
  • Mamma Mia! Here We Go Again

Sam Skarsgård’s Siblings

Apart from Sam, all his siblings have already started a career in film acting. His siblings are Alexander Skarsgard, his oldest brother, who first gained international recognition for portraying Eric Northman in HBO’s fantasy drama ‘True Blood’ (2008-14). He has also appeared in films like ‘Melancholia,’ ‘What Maisie Knew’, ‘The Legend of Tarzan’ and ‘Hold the Dark.

His next brother, Gustaf, who is younger than Alexander but older than Sam, is currently portraying Floki in History Channel’s ‘Vikings’ (2013-present). Bill Skarsgård, Sam’s younger brother, is also an actor and has starred in films like the dystopian thriller ‘The Divergent Series: Allegiant and the espionage thriller ‘Atomic Blonde.’ Valter Skarsgård is the youngest child of Stellan and My. He has also appeared in films like ‘To Kill a Child,’ ‘Arn – The Knight Templar’, and ‘Arn – The Kingdom at Road’s End. Sam’s only sister, Eija, is a former model.
