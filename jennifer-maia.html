Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Jennifer Maia is a talented mixed martial artist (MMA artist). At present, this personality competes in the Women’s Flyweight division. As such, she competes in the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). This MMA artist trains at Chute Boxe Academy in Brazil. She turned professional in the year 2009. Moreover, this personality is the former Invicta FC Flyweight World Champion. As of 12 April 2022, Maia is #7 in the UFC women’s flyweight rankings.

Talking about her career in boxing, this personality made her professional Boxing debut on 29 March 2008. She did so at Centri de Boxe in Curitiba, Brazil. For this, this personality faced Michelle Bonassoli in an 8-round bout that saw this personality victorious via unanimous decision.

Likewise, this personality’s 2nd bout was against Juliana de Aguiar. This was another 8-round bout in which Maia won via points. Similarly, Maia’s last boxing match was with Lorena Nancy Lopez on 5 August 2009. It was before her transitioning into MMA.

Furthermore, this personality made her professional MMA debut on 5 December 2009. The MMA artist won her initial four fights finishing each one in the 1st round with three submissions and one TKO. However, on 28 October 2011, this MMA artist suffered her 1st loss to fellow Brazilian Vanessa Porto. This match was at Kumite MMA Combate.
