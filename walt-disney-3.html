Walt Disney aka Walter Elias Disney was an American animator, producer and entrepreneur. As of 1966, Walt Disney’s net worth is $1 billion. He established many innovations in the creation of cartoons as a pioneer of the American animation business.

He holds the record for the most Academy Awards won by a person as a film producer, having won 22 Oscars from 59 nominations. Among other accolades, he received two Golden Globe Special Achievement Awards and an Emmy Award.
