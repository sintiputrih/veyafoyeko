Is Dr Disrespect the Tallest Streamer in Twitch?

Dr Disrespect previously held the title of the tallest streamer on Twitch, but he’s not alone.

Skilled Dota 2 player ‘Saksa’ shares the same towering height of 6 feet 8 inches (approx. 2.03m).

Saksa now claims the ‘Tallest Twitch Streamer’ title due to Dr Disrespect’s absence from Twitch.

In an intriguing coincidence, both Saksa and Dr Disrespect stand at 6 feet 8 inches, an extraordinary height.
