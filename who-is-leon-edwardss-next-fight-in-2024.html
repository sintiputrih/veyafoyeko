In 2024, the UFC welterweight division is abuzz with speculation and anticipation surrounding who will be the next opponent for the reigning champion, Leon Edwards.

With a string of impressive victories and a twelve-fight unbeaten streak, Edwards has solidified his position as one of the top welterweights in the world.

As fans eagerly await his next challenge, let's delve into the potential opponents and the landscape of the welterweight division to explore who might step into the Octagon to face "Rocky" Edwards in 2024.

Current State of the Welterweight Division

The welterweight division in the UFC is known for its depth and competitive nature, with a roster of talented fighters vying for a shot at the title.

As the reigning champion, Leon Edwards has established himself as a formidable force in the division, showcasing his technical prowess and resilience inside the Octagon.

Recent Performances and Rankings

  • Leon Edwards: With an impressive twelve-fight unbeaten streak, including victories over top contenders, Edwards has proven himself as a dominant force in the welterweight division. His recent retention of the welterweight title against Colby Covington at UFC 296 further solidified his position at the top of the division.

  • Belal Muhammad: Belal Muhammad, currently ranked as the No. 2 contender in the welterweight division, is widely considered to be the next man in line for a shot at Edwards' welterweight gold.

  • Khamzat Chimaev: The undefeated Chechen prospect, Khamzat Chimaev, has been earmarked as a potential opponent for Edwards. Chimaev's meteoric rise and dominant performances have garnered attention, positioning him as a formidable challenger in the division.

  • Shavkat Rakhmonov: Shavkat Rakhmonov, another rising star in the welterweight division, presents an intriguing option as a potential opponent for Edwards. Rakhmonov's impressive undefeated record and notable victories have placed him in the spotlight as a contender for the title.
